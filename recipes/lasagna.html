Cheese lasagna

Cheese lasagna recipe

Ingredients

    Lasagna:

  • 500 g of mozzarella cheese (sliced).
  • 1 Package (400 g) of fresh dough for lasagna.
  • Grated Parmesan cheese at taste.
  • 1 Sachet (340g) of tomato sauce.

    • White sauce:

  • 750 ml of milk.
  • 2 Spoons (soup) of maize starch (cornstarch).
  • 1 Cube of chicken broth.
  • 1 Spoon (soup) of butter.
  • Salt at taste.
  • Nutmeg at taste.
  • Basil at taste.

Steps

    How to prepare the white sauce:

  1. On a pan, put the butter and let it melt over low heat;
  2. Then put the milk, mix and add remaining of ingredients;
  3. Mix until it thickens;
  4. The last step, add the nutmeg and the basil, take the salt, mix and turnoff the fire;

    5. How to prepare the assembly:

  5. Spread a little of tomato sauce in a platter;
  6. Make a layer with a lasagna dough, a layer of mozzarella and cover with a little more white sauce;
  7. Then make more one layer with lasagna dough, mozzarella, tomato sauce and white sauce until it's over;
  8. Keep interspersing until you run out;
  9. Ending sprinkle grated parmesan cheese over;
  10. Cover the platter with aluminum paper;
  11. Then bake in preheated oven, 180ÂºC, for 30 at 40 minutes;
  12. Remove of oven and serve next.
