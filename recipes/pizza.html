Pizza

Pizza recipe for wood oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg of wheat flour
  • 30 g of fresh biological yeast (for bread)
  • 500 ml of water
  • 6 g or 1 spoon (dessert) of refined sugar
  • 5 g or 1 spoon (tea) of salt
  • 12 g or 1 spoon (soup) of margarine/butter
  • 30 ml or 2 spoons (soup) of oil

Steps

  1. Place the fresh biological yeast in a container with a little water, add the sugar and dissolve well. Place the wheat flour in another container or in a table and add the salt, make an opening in center and add the dissolved fresh biological yeast. Add the oil and the margarine/butter and mix well with fingers and add the remaining water. Mix all until turning a dough and beat for approximately 15 minutes.
  2. Place the ready dough, to rest for 2 hours in a container sprinkled with wheat flour or place the dough in 4 little balls format in a table sprinkled with wheat flour.
  3. The dough will grow a little and after is only opening the pizzas with a roll sprinkled ever with wheat flour.
  4. If you place to rest in a container, cut the 4 pieces and make little ball before opening the dough with a roll.
  5. After is only take the tomato's sauce of you preference, and fill to taste, can you make the edge stuffed, making the disk bigger.
  6. Yields 4 Big Pizzas.
